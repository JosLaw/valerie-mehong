About

Coming from Reunion Island, Valérie moved to England and joined Nine Tails Tattoo Studio as a receptionist in 2017. This grew her interest and love of japanese style tattoos. Inspired by the resident tattooers and amazing guest artists, she started her apprenticeship under shop owner Kanae in 2018. In 2022 she became a resident artist at Ninetails Tattoo Studio in Shorditch, London, and Evil from the Needle Tattoo Studio in Camden, London. Despite her main style being Japanese, she is open to other styles if requested.
